Full Home Inspection:


From the foundation to the rooftop, we inspect your entire home according to the most recent Standards of Practise as set by the American Society of Home Inspectors. The following home systems are inspected:

Click below tabs for further information

    Roof inspection covers the following:
  • -Flashing.
  • -Roofing materials.
  • -Roof drainage systems.
  • -Skylights, chimneys, and roof penetrations.
    • We will describe the following:
  • -Roofing materials.
  • -Methods used to inspect the roofing.

    Exterior inspection covers the following:
  • -Exterior Doors.
  • -Wall coverings, flashing and trim.
  • -Eaves, soffits and fascias accessible from the ground level.
  • -Adjacent and entryway walkways, patios and driveways.
  • -Attached and adjacent decks, balconies stoops,steps, porches, and their associated railings.
  • -Vegetation, grading, surface drainage, and retaining walls that are likely to affect the building adversely.
    • We will describe the following:
  • -Wall coverings.

    Plumbing inspection covers the following:
  • -Vent systems, flues and chimneys.
  • -Fuel storage and fuel distribution systems.
  • -Sewage ejectors, sump pumps, and related piping.
  • -Water heating equipment and hot water supply systems.
  • -Interior drain, waste and vent systems, including fixtures.
  • -Interior water supply and distribution systems, including fixtures and faucets.
    • We will describe the following:
  • -Interior water supply, drain, waste and vent piping materials.
  • -Water heating equipment, including energy sources(s).
  • -Location of main water and fuel shut-off valves.

    Electrical inspection covers the following:
  • -Service drop.
  • -Conductors.
  • -Service Grounding.
  • -Overcurrent protection devices.
  • -Service equipment and main disconnects.
  • -Service entrance conductors, cables and raceways.
  • -Interior components of service panels and subpanels.
  • -Ground fault circuit interruptors and arc fault circuit interrupters.
  • -A representative number of installed lighting fixtures, switches and receptacles. We will describe the following:
  • -The amperage rating of the service.
  • -Location of main disconnects(s) and subpanels.
  • -The predominant branch circuit wiring method.
  • -Presence or absense of smoke alarms and carbon monoxide alarms.

    Heating and Cooling inspection covers the following:
  • -Distribution systems.
  • -Open readily openable access panels.
  • -Vent systems, flues and chimneys.
  • -Central and permanently installed cooling equipment.
    • We will describe the following:
  • -Energy sources(s).
  • -Heating systems.
  • -Cooling systems.

    Interior inspection covers the following:
  • -Walls, ceilings and floors.
  • -Steps, stairways and railings.
  • -A representative number of doors and windows.
  • -Garage vehicle doors and garage vehicle door operators.
  • -Countertops and a representative number of installed cabinets.
  • -Installed ovens, ranges surface cooking appliances, microwave ovens, dishwashing machines, and food waste grinders by using normal operating controls to activate the primary function.

    Insulation and Ventilation inspection covers the following:
  • -Clothes dryer exhaust systems.
  • -Ventilation of attics and foundation areas.
  • -Insulation and vapor retarders in unfinished spaces.
  • -Kitchen, bathroom, laundry and similar exhaust systems.
    • We will describe the following:
  • -Insulation and vapor retarders in unfinished spaces.
  • -Absence of insulation in unfinished spaces at condition surfaces.

    Appliance inspection covers the following:
  • -Installed appliances such as dishwasher, oven, refrigerator and laundry machines.
